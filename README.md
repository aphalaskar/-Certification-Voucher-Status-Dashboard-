# -Certification-Voucher-Status-Dashboard-
This Power BI report provides an end‑to‑end analysis of certification voucher utilization across the organization for the years 2023 and 2024. It enables leadership and L&amp;D teams to track certification progress, employee engagement, and business line participation.
1. Date & Year Filters
Slicers for month and year (2023 & 2024) allow users to filter the report dynamically.
Useful for month‑wise, quarter‑wise, or yearly analysis.

2. Business Line Analysis
A clustered bar chart shows the distribution of employees across business lines, including:
AIM & Data Engineering
GEN AI and Next‑Gen Analytics
Central Functions
This visual helps understand which business units are most active in certification programs.

4. Total Employee Count
A KPI card displaying:
1487 Total Employees tracked in the certification initiative.
   
4. Hyperscaler vs Non‑Hyperscaler Certifications
Shows how many employees pursued:
Hyperscaler certifications (e.g., AWS, Azure, GCP)
Non‑Hyperscaler certifications
This helps L&D teams identify cloud‑related upskilling trends.

6. Exam Status
A bar chart summarizing progress across certification stages:
Not Started
Scheduled
Certified
Not Certified
Not Scheduled
This visual highlights pipeline readiness and completion rates.

6. Certification Name Breakdown
A horizontal bar chart listing certification names (DP‑203, AZ‑900, etc.) with corresponding completion counts.
Useful for understanding high‑demand certifications.
